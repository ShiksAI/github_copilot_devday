# Social Bingo Design Spec

## Overview
Redesign the Social Bingo web app with a playful, fun aesthetic. Transform it into an engaging, colorful experience with animations, emojis, and vibrant colors to make the bingo game delightful and social.

## Design Goals
- **Playful & Fun**: Vibrant colors, emojis, animations
- **Engaging**: Interactive elements, visual feedback
- **Social**: Emphasize the social aspect of finding matching people
- **Accessible**: Maintain usability while adding fun elements

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Bright pink (#FF6B9D) for accents and buttons
- **Secondary**: Turquoise (#4ECDC4) for highlights
- **Background**: Soft gradient from light yellow (#FFF9C4) to light pink (#FCE4EC)
- **Marked squares**: Bright green (#81C784) with confetti effect
- **Winning squares**: Gold (#FFD54F) with sparkle animation
- **Text**: Dark purple (#4A148C) for readability

## Typography
- **Font**: Fun, rounded font (Google Fonts: "Fredoka One" or similar)
- **Emojis**: Integrate emojis in questions and UI elements
- **Sizes**: Larger, bolder text for headings

## Animations & Interactions
- **Button hover**: Scale and color change
- **Square marking**: Bounce animation + confetti particles
- **Bingo modal**: Bounce in with celebration animation
- **Winning line**: Pulse and glow effect
- **Page transitions**: Smooth fades
